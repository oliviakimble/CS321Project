TimeAlign

Shift-Management System


Who Are We?

We are individuals dedicated to helping businesses save time, improve workforce management efficiency, increase employee engagement, and ease workload via a seamless scheduling system.

What Inspired Us?

The problem with the time and complexity of shift management in businesses with large numbers of employees, unhappy employees due to disorganized scheduling. Other employee management systems do not place the level of focus that we do on employee satisfaction. We want to make sure your employees are happy with their schedules while simultaneously helping managers save any time with scheduling large numbers of employees.

What is Time Align?

Each employee has their own account with their employer information which can be used to input their job position, availability, contact information, and level of expertise/knowledge in an easy-to-read week-by-week format. The data from these unique profiles can then be used by managers to be able to schedule their employees on shifts more quickly and in a way that suits each individual employee the best. Employees will have the option to “release shift” or “trade shift” with a selected Data from user profiles can be laid out for managers and employers in a customizable, easy-to-read format. It essentially allows them to view any piece of information about any number of employees in an overlaid style/cumulative format. An example would be if managers wanted to view all employees with a particular job title and view all of their week-to-week availabilities at once.
